About Dr. Johnson

Dr Johnson is a Husband, Father, Doctor and an Entrepreneur. After completing six years of medical studies, Dr Johnson has been involved in the medical industry for a further 30 years. During this time, Dr. Johnson has transitioned from a General Medical Practitioner to assisting with the development and roll-out of patient management programs, to developing a web-based medical recruitment company and to founding a flourishing health and wellness company.

Dr. Johnson involvement in the wellness industry has exposed him to the magnitude of undetected chronic diseases within the general population. This has led him to develop systematic approaches to the detection and management of chronic diseases.
